Biography

My name is Obatola Opeyemi, and I come from a family of five, where I am the eldest of three siblings. I am 26 years old and studied Transport Planning and Management at Olabisi Onabanjo University.

I earned my bachelor's degree in 2022 and then completed my National Youth Service in 2024. During my service year, I was deployed to Ogun State, where, after my orientation program, I was posted to Christ's Mercy Comprehensive College in Oke-Saje for my primary assignment.

While serving, I joined the Nigeria Red Cross Society at the Abeokuta-North branch, where I took on the role of Public Relations Officer. I participated in several public speaking engagements during this time.

I also held leadership positions as the president of the Winners' Youth Corpers Lodge and the Winners' Youth Corpers Fellowship (WYCF).

Currently, I work as a Sales Executive at Zedvance Finance. I joined the company as a graduate trainee and am also enrolled at Altschool Africa's School of Engineering, which I am funding myself.

I joined the School of Engineering program for three key reasons

  • Access to Global Opportunities: The tech industry has transformed the world into one connected global marketplace. I’m venturing into tech to tap into these endless global opportunities — to work on impactful projects, collaborate with professionals across borders, and build a career without geographical limits.
  • Career Switch: I decided to switch to tech because of its dynamic nature, constant growth, and the ability to solve real-world problems. Engineering offers me a structured pathway to pivot into this industry with the right technical foundation.
  • Skills Advancement: Continuous learning and improvement are important to me. Joining this program is a step toward acquiring advanced, in-demand technical skills that will make me relevant and competitive in today’s fast-evolving tech landscape.

My Goals for the School of Engineering Program

  • Build Strong Technical Foundations: I aim to gain a deep understanding of core engineering and technology concepts that will help me solve real-world problems confidently.
  • Master In-Demand Tech Skills: My goal is to develop proficiency in key areas such as software development, problem-solving, and engineering best practices, making me competitive in the global job market.
  • Gain Hands-On Experience: I plan to work on projects, collaborate with peers, and participate in challenges that will help me apply what I learn and build an impressive project portfolio.
  • Expand My Network: I want to connect with like-minded individuals, mentors, and industry professionals who can guide me and open doors to future opportunities.
  • Prepare for Global Opportunities: By the end of the program, I hope to position myself for global roles and internships, showcasing the skills and mindset required for international tech careers.
  • Contribute and Give Back: As I grow, I also aim to mentor others, share knowledge, and contribute to the tech community to inspire and support future learners.
